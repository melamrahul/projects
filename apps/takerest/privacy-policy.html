Privacy Policy

Effective date: January 1, 2025 · Last updated: January 1, 2025

Overview

Take Rest is a screen-time management application designed to help you rest better by locking your device on a schedule.

Take Rest collects no personal data and operates completely offline on your device.

Data we collect

Take Rest does not collect, store, or transmit any personal information.

All preferences, schedules, and settings are stored locally on your device using Android's secure storage system.

Permissions we use

  • Device Administrator — Used only to lock your device screen.
  • RECEIVE_BOOT_COMPLETED — Restarts scheduled locks after device reboot.
  • USE_EXACT_ALARM — Ensures lock schedules run at exact times.
  • FOREGROUND_SERVICE — Keeps the lock service running reliably.
  • POST_NOTIFICATIONS — Shows lock status and remaining time.

Third-party services

Take Rest does not use advertising services, analytics tools, crash reporting tools, or cloud services.

Data security

All data remains on your device. Security is handled by Android's application sandbox and device security features.

Children's privacy

Take Rest does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. The application is safe for all users.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy occasionally. Any updates will be reflected by changing the effective date above.

Contact

If you have questions regarding this privacy policy, please contact us through the GitHub repository or the app store listing.
