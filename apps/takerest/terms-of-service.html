What Take Rest Does

Take Rest is a screen-time management tool designed to help users build healthier digital habits by locking their device during scheduled periods or on demand.

  • Quick Lock — instantly lock your device for a chosen duration
  • Schedule Lock — automatically lock the device during recurring time windows
  • Tab Protection — delay-based PIN protection to prevent accidental changes
  • App Lock PIN — restricts access to Take Rest settings

Device Administrator Permission

Take Rest requests Device Administrator permission only to lock your screen using Android's DevicePolicyManager API.

  • The permission is used only to call lockNow()
  • The app cannot wipe, track, or remotely manage your device
  • You can revoke this permission anytime through device settings
⚠️ If you enable Admin Protection inside the app, you must disable that option within Take Rest before removing the Device Administrator permission.
